Vaccination Product

A virtual concept, derived from the vaccination admin code and the vaccination manufacturer. The vaccine CVX/CPT code and the vaccine manufacturer, together, identify a specific vaccine product that was administered. If a vaccination product is not recognized, this indicates that the manufacturer and the vaccine CVX/CPT were not recognized as a valid combination. In this case the solution is to ensure that the correct CVX/CPT or MVX is being used. It is important to remember:

  • CVX codes are preferred over CPT. If both are sent, CVX is normally used and the CPT ignored.
  • The date of the vaccination affects which combinations are considered valid. (Vaccine Products are only valid around and including the dates they were manufacturered by a particular manufacturer.)
  • Vaccine products are deprecated when they are known to be valid values but have been administered earlier or later than when known to be manufactured. This may happen during transitions from one manufacturer code to another (such as when one manufacturer merges with another.) Please keep manufacturer codes up-to-date.

Vaccine products are currently defined by the CDC.

Important Note: Issues associated with this field are only triggered if the vaccination is indicates has having been administered. Historical vaccinations are not expected to be reported with this information.
